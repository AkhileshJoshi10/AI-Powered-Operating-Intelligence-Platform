# Problem Statement

Modern businesses generate large amounts of data from different departments such as sales, inventory, finance, customer support, vendors, procurement, HR, and operations. However, this data is often scattered across spreadsheets, dashboards, emails, reports, and business systems.

Managers usually depend on manual analysis, delayed reports, and traditional dashboards to understand business performance. These dashboards show what has already happened, but they do not clearly explain why a problem occurred, which issue should be handled first, what action should be taken, or how follow-up work should be automated.

Because of this, decision-making becomes slow, important problems may be missed, and managers spend significant time coordinating between different teams.

This project aims to solve this problem by developing an AI-Powered Operating Intelligence Platform that uses a multi-agent AI system to monitor business data, detect important issues, prioritize problems, explain root causes, recommend actions, and automate workflows using tools such as n8n.
