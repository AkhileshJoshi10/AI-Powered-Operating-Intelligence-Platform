## KPI Definitions

- **Total Sales:** Total value of all completed sales transactions.
- **Total Revenue:** Total revenue recorded in the finance reporting table.
- **Sales Growth:** Month-on-month change in total sales for the latest month.
- **Store Target Achievement:** Weighted target achievement across all stores in the latest available finance month.
- **Average Order Value:** Average sales value per transaction.
- **Total Operating Profit:** Total operating profit after operating expenses.
- **Low-Stock Count:** Number of inventory records marked as Low Stock.
- **Overstock Count:** Number of inventory records marked as Overstock.
- **High-Severity Complaint Count:** Total complaints classified as High severity.
- **Open Complaint Count:** Complaints that remain Open or In Progress.
- **Vendor Delay Count:** Number of vendor deliveries received after the scheduled delivery date.
- **Vendor On-Time Delivery Rate:** Percentage of deliveries received on or before the scheduled delivery date.
