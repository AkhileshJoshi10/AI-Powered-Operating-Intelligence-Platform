# Project Objectives

## Main Objective

To design and develop an AI-Powered Operating Intelligence Platform that uses a multi-agent AI system to monitor business data, identify important issues, support managerial decision-making, and automate follow-up workflows.

## Detailed Objective

The objective of this project is to build an intelligent business support system that can collect and analyze business data, detect performance issues, prioritize problems, explain root causes, generate AI-based recommendations, and automate routine managerial workflows.

The platform will act like an AI Chief of Staff for managers by helping them understand what is happening in the business, why it is happening, what should be done next, and who should take action.

## Sub-Objectives

1. To collect and process business data from sources such as sales, inventory, finance, customer complaints, vendors, contracts, and operations.

2. To monitor key business indicators and detect important issues such as sales decline, inventory shortage, complaint increase, vendor delay, or financial risk.

3. To prioritize detected business problems based on urgency, impact, and severity.

4. To generate root cause explanations for major business problems.

5. To provide AI-generated recommendations with suggested action, responsible owner, deadline, and expected impact.

6. To create a manager review screen where recommendations can be accepted, edited, rejected, or assigned.

7. To maintain a Kanban board for tracking tasks as unassigned, ongoing, or completed.

8. To integrate workflow automation using n8n for alerts, emails, reminders, task updates, and follow-ups.

9. To develop a dashboard that displays KPIs, alerts, priority issues, recommendations, and automation status.

10. To demonstrate how AI agents can support business intelligence, decision support, and workflow automation in a practical business environment.
