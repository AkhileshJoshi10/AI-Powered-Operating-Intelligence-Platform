# Demo Company

## Company Name

SmartMart Retail Pvt. Ltd.

## Company Type

Retail / FMCG Superstore Business

## Company Description

SmartMart Retail Pvt. Ltd. is a fictional retail and FMCG superstore company. The company sells products across multiple categories such as groceries, personal care, household items, packaged food, beverages, electronics accessories, and daily-use consumer goods.

The company operates through multiple stores and regional distribution channels. It manages sales, inventory, customer complaints, supplier deliveries, distributor performance, product categories, and financial transactions.

## Why This Company Is Selected

SmartMart Retail Pvt. Ltd. is selected because a retail/FMCG superstore business provides a strong and practical use case for this project. It has multiple business functions where data is generated regularly and business problems can be clearly identified.

This company is suitable because it can easily show issues such as:

- Sales decline
- Product underperformance
- Inventory shortage
- Overstocking
- Customer complaints
- Supplier/vendor delays
- Payment delays
- Store-wise performance issues
- Region-wise sales variation
- Category-wise performance problems

## Why Retail/FMCG Is Best for This Project

Retail and FMCG businesses are ideal for this project because their data is easier to collect, generate, and analyze. Public datasets related to retail sales, inventory, products, stores, and supply chain are also more easily available compared to highly specific industries.

This business type allows the project to demonstrate business monitoring, KPI tracking, issue detection, priority ranking, root cause analysis, recommendation generation, task tracking, and workflow automation in a clear and realistic way.

## Use in Project

The sample data of SmartMart Retail Pvt. Ltd. will be used to demonstrate how the AI-Powered Operating Intelligence Platform can:

1. Monitor business KPIs
2. Track sales performance
3. Analyze inventory levels
4. Detect important business issues
5. Prioritize problems based on urgency and impact
6. Explain possible root causes
7. Generate AI-based recommendations
8. Suggest action owner, deadline, and expected impact
9. Create tasks for managers
10. Automate follow-up workflows using n8n

## Final Note

SmartMart Retail Pvt. Ltd. is a fictional company created only for project demonstration purposes. It will help show how the AI-Powered Operating Intelligence Platform can work in a realistic retail/FMCG business environment.
